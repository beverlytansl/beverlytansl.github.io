entrepreneurship

For Old Times' Sake (FOTS) is an ecommerce start up that sells accessories such as earrings and necklaces. Our brand believes in collective collaboration within the community. Just like the Old Times' shared between friends, our brand believes in having fun creating and experimenting, while still looking chic and trendy.

FOTS's business model is based on 2 product ranges, accessories that are designed by myself, as well as customs, accessories that are designed by the individual. I have sold close to 400 pairs of earrings and necklaces since it's inception in November 2018. FOTS operates mainly in Singapore, with our target audience being youths from age 18-24. However, FOTS has also had customers from Australia, America, Indonesia and Thailand.

I also invest heavily in advertising, marketing and PR relations to maintain and enhance my brand awareness. The critical association that I try to foster is unique jewellery made especially for the individual and accessories that are trendy.

FOTS also keep a tight control on the supply chain, by sourcing the best products and internalized manufacturing to have as much control as possible on the quality of the final outcome.
